About This Project

This is a simple "AI-powered" Tic-Tac-Toe game built using Pygame and the Minimax Algorithm. The AI can be adjusted to different difficulty levels.

Features

  • AI opponent with Minimax Algorithm
  • Three difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard)
  • Graphical interface using Pygame

How to Play?

Simply click on a square to place your move. The AI will respond based on the difficulty setting. The game will announce a winner or a draw, and you can restart by clicking anywhere.
